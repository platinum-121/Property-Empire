# Property Empire Simulator Design

## Core Fantasy
Start with a small property company and build a portfolio across major American cities.

## Core Loop
1. Review city and building economics.
2. Buy properties with cash or loans.
3. Collect monthly rent.
4. Pay maintenance, taxes, payroll, and loan interest.
5. Use financial statements to understand performance.
6. Expand into more attractive property markets.
7. Watch competitors buy portfolios and grow.

## Active Systems
- Property portfolio
- Cities and countries
- Company cash
- Loans and credit rating
- Income statement, balance sheet, cash flow
- News feed
- NPC property competitors
- Save/load

## Disabled Direction
Banking operations, stock markets, multiple industries, research trees, and complex subsidiaries are outside the current game direction.
